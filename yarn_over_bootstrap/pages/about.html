About Yarn Over

Welcome to Yarn Over, your one-stop shop for all things crochet! Our store is proudly owned and operated by Annette and Katie, two lifelong friends and passionate crocheters. Both Annette and Katie learned to crochet at a young age but rediscovered their love for the craft in their mid-20s. Since then, they’ve been inspired by the endless possibilities crochet offers, creating unique pieces that showcase their hard work and dedication.

Fueled by their passion for the art of crochet and a shared dream of becoming entrepreneurs, Annette and Katie poured their hearts into building Yarn Over. Their vision was simple: to create a welcoming space for people who just love to crochet! Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced crafter, Yarn Over is here to support your creativity, provide high-quality supplies, and celebrate the crochet community.

Annette and Katie also believe in giving back to the community that supports their business. They strive to make Yarn Over more than just a store—it’s a hub for creativity, connection, and learning. From hosting classes and monthly showcases to offering personalized advice and inspiration, they aim to help, teach, and provide for crocheters of all skill levels.

Annette and Katie’s journey has been one of hard work and dedication. They are incredibly proud of what they’ve accomplished and the community they’ve built around Yarn Over. Whether you’re looking to take a class, browse for supplies, or simply connect with other crochet enthusiasts, Yarn Over is here for you.
