Yarn Over and Learn Crochet

At Yarn Over, we’re excited to share our love of crocheting with our community through fun and educational classes and events! We offer bi-weekly crochet classes every other Wednesday night at 6:00 PM, designed for everyone—from beginners just picking up a hook to advanced crocheters looking to refine their skills. Our classes cover everything from basic stitches and reading patterns to more advanced techniques like creating intricate designs and working with specialty yarns. Whether you’re learning the basics or tackling challenging projects, there’s always something new to discover.

For the little ones, we host monthly kids’ crochet classes on Saturdays at 2:00 PM. These classes are perfect for inspiring creativity and teaching foundational skills in a fun, hands-on environment. Kids will learn to make simple projects like bracelets, small toys, and colorful patches, giving them a sense of pride and accomplishment. It’s a great way to introduce them to the joy of crafting!

Don’t miss our artist showcase events held on the first Sunday of every month. These gatherings are a wonderful opportunity for crocheters to display their hard work and creativity. From cozy blankets to intricate lacework, the showcase is all about celebrating the unique talents of our community. Visitors can browse, connect with other crafters, and even gain inspiration for their own projects.

Join us for our classes and events, and become part of the Yarn Over community. Let’s create, learn, and celebrate together!
