Yarn Over Showcase

At Yarn Over, we’re passionate about celebrating the art of crochet and the talented individuals who bring it to life. That’s why we’re thrilled to host our monthly Crochet Showcase on the first Sunday of every month! This special event is open to everyone—whether you’re an experienced crocheter, a beginner looking for inspiration, or simply an admirer of handmade artistry.

The Crochet Showcase is a space for local crocheters to proudly display their finished projects, from cozy blankets and intricate lacework to charming amigurumi and wearable creations. It’s not just about showing off your work—it’s about connecting with others who share your passion. Customers taking our classes can use this opportunity to network, share tips, and celebrate their progress. There’s no better place to feel proud of what you’ve made and inspire others to try something new.

As a thank-you for supporting our community, we’re offering one free cup of coffee and a free skein of yarn with any in-store purchase made during the showcase. It’s the perfect way to fuel your creativity and stock up on supplies for your next project.

So, mark your calendar and join us for our next Crochet Showcase! Whether you’re participating or just stopping by to appreciate the incredible work on display, we’d love to see you there. Let’s celebrate the joy of creating, sharing, and connecting—together at Yarn Over.
