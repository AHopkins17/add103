Yarn Over and Shop

Crochet Supplies

Don’t have time to crochet but love handmade items? Yarn Over offers a variety of custom, handcrafted crochet creations made with care by our talented team. From scarves and hats to home décor and toys, each piece is lovingly crafted to suit your preferences. Looking for something truly unique? Place a custom order, and we’ll create a one-of-a-kind item just for you or as a perfect gift.

Yarn

Discover a vibrant array of yarns in our store, perfect for projects big and small! We carry a variety of textures, weights, and fibers, including cotton, wool, acrylic, and blended options, ensuring you find the perfect match for any creation. With an expansive selection of colors, from bold and bright to soft and subtle, the possibilities are endless. Don’t know where to start? Our knowledgeable staff is happy to help you choose the right yarn for your next masterpiece.

Patterns

Looking for inspiration? Browse our diverse collection of patterns for projects of all types and skill levels. Whether you want to create cozy blankets, stylish garments, or fun amigurumi, we have patterns to spark your creativity. Choose from digital or printed formats and enjoy detailed instructions designed to guide you every step of the way. Let us help you turn your ideas into tangible, beautiful pieces.
