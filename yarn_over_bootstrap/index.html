Yarn Over: A Place for Crochet

We are thrilled to announce that we are officially OPEN FOR BUSINESS! Whether you’re a seasoned crocheter or just starting your journey, Yarn Over is here to meet all your crochet needs.
Explore our store for an extensive collection of crochet supplies, including high-quality hooks, hook sets, and accessories. With numerous sizes, colors, and materials to choose from, you’ll find exactly what you need for your next project.
Looking for inspiration? Browse our wide variety of patterns or let us create something truly unique for you with a custom design, all handmade by our passionate owners.
But that’s not all!

At Yarn Over, we believe in creating a welcoming and inspiring space for crochet lovers. Relax with a fresh cup of coffee and enjoy our delicious baked goods while you work on your latest creation. Take a seat in one of our comfy chairs, couches, or worktables designed with crafters in mind.

We’re proud to offer bi-weekly crochet classes for beginners through advanced learners, as well as monthly kids’ classes to inspire the next generation of crocheters. Plus, don’t miss our artist showcase events, where we celebrate the incredible work of fellow enthusiasts and provide a platform for crafters to share their talent.
Ready to join the Yarn Over community? Stop by our store or visit our shop page to see what we have in store. Watch for exclusive deals and sales coming soon!
